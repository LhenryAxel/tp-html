Golden State Warriors

Home: San Francisco, California

Championships: 7

The Golden State Warriors are one of the most dominant teams of the 21st century, known for their revolutionary style of play and led by stars like Stephen Curry, Klay Thompson, and Draymond Green. They are credited with popularizing the three-point shot and have won multiple championships under coach Steve Kerr.

Notable Players

  • Stephen Curry
  • Klay Thompson
  • Kevin Durant (2016-2019)
  • Draymond Green
  • Wilt Chamberlain (1959-1965)

Achievements

  • 7 NBA Championships
  • Most regular season wins in a single season (73-9 in 2016)
  • 5-time Western Conference Champions (2015, 2016, 2017, 2018, 2019)

Fun Facts

  • Stephen Curry holds the record for most three-pointers in NBA history.
  • The Warriors were founded in 1946 as the Philadelphia Warriors.
  • Golden State's team colors are blue and yellow, symbolizing California's state colors.
